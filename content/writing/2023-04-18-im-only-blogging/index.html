Well, it’s April 2023 and I promised myself this year I’d try to be a bit more creative and do a bit of writing. It’ll mostly be nonsense and no doubt a poor imitation of the writers I love but you can only hope to write songs as good as your record collection.

Anyway the name of my website comes from the great Bob Dylan song: It’s Alright Ma (I’m Only Bleeding). So I thought it would be a nice way to begin by trying to write another three verses of this masterpiece but with a modern twist.

Silent screens dictate the rules
as people seek solace in the cyber cesspool
Chasing hits and likes from online ghouls
Faking content to make their users drool
Forsake themselves for digital jewels
and wonder why they feel empty

For the market’s hunger knows no bounds
it consumes the soul as greed is crowned
in a plastic palace raised from the ground
While the endless race to grow and compound
Makes it impossible for the truth to be found
That enough is more than plenty

In this age of noise, where silence flees
We shout for meaning, drowning in the seas
of shallow thoughts and easy tease
we drift apart with inevitable ease
yet before the screens we’re on our knees
praying for an iPhone 20

But don’t mind too much what I write
it’s only a feather in a cannon fight
It’s alright, Ma, I’m only blogging

Apologies to all Dylanolgists out there!
